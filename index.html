What can I do here?

Imagine that you are in a store and you want to buy a socks. You find three different and favourite colors of socks. You have no idea which one you should choose. That’s a hard dilemma. And this is the right situation to use ‘Conflicted’! This tool allows you to write in anything you want: socks, movies, next color of your hair, etc… and make decision for you. You don’t have to agree with everything the tool will show, you can say ‘yes’ or ‘no’. It always a beginning!
